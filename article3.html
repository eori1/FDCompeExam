𝗚𝗿𝗮𝗱𝘂𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝘀𝘁𝘂𝗱𝗲𝗻𝘁𝘀 𝘂𝗿𝗴𝗲𝗱 𝘁𝗼 𝘀𝗲𝗰𝘂𝗿𝗲 𝗔𝗹𝘂𝗺𝗻𝗶 𝗜𝗗 𝗯𝗲𝗳𝗼𝗿𝗲 𝗴𝗿𝗮𝗱𝘂𝗮𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻

Graduating students at the University were encouraged to register for their alumni identification cards prior to this year's Commencement Exercises on June 13.

The alumni ID card will be used during the graduates' oath taking-ceremony to induct them as an official alumnus of the University.

Students will be required to fill out a form and pay a one-time fee of P500 for membership at the West Visayas State University Federated Alumni Association Inc. Office and an additional P100 for printing.

Taga-West may access the Membership Form through the link below: https://forms.gle/rDxeE3oaBbgzW2T86

Story by Symon Peterneil Vacunawa
