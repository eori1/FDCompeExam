PANGINBULAHAN

Forum-Dimensions extends its warmest congratulations to Nove Joy Losbañes, Editor-in-Chief for academic year 2024-2025, for being this year's recipient of the esteemed Esteban Javellana Award for Excellence in the Field of Creative Writing.

Nove began her journey with the Publication in 2021, later becoming as one of the News and Special Reports Editors, Managing Editor, and now as Chief Editor.

This distinction honors a senior student who has shown exceptional talent in creative writing and made significant contributions through published works like essays, features, plays, poems, and stories.

This marks the third consecutive year a Forum-Dimensions staff member has brought home the award.

"Sa bansang ito, kapag lumalaban ka parang nagiging apoy ka. Sa una ay nagbabaga, ngunit pilit inaapula hanggang sa maging abo ka na." — "Apoy at Abo" by Nove Joy Losbañes

Congratulations, Ate Nove! May you never lose the fire in your pen, no matter what trials come your way.
